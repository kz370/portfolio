CIC Official Website: Performance at Scale.

Managing the high-traffic public face of the university with a widely optimized WordPress architecture.

The Challenge

Fragmented Data, Operational Chaos.

Prior to this system, the university operated on disjointed islands of data: WordPress for the front, legacy SQL for grades, and external providers for payments.

My Role: My focus was on Content Delivery & Reliability. I optimized the WordPress theme and database to handle massive traffic spikes during exam result announcements and enrollment periods without downtime.
