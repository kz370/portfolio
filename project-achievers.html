Performance Tracking Platform

We Achieve: Gamifying Professional Growth.

A dedicated platform for tracking user goals, challenges, and progress with complex filament dashboards and community features.

The Challenge

Organizations need a way to track "soft" metrics like personal goals and community challenges alongside hard data.

Technical Implementation:

  • Filament Dashboard

    Leveraged FilamentPHP for rapid, beautiful admin panels to manage Users, Goals, and Challenges.

  • Community Logic

    Built complex relationships including User Communities, Badges (pivot tables), and Social Login (Google/FB).

  • Auth Architecture

    Implemented secure authentication flows, CRUD operations for goals, and progress tracking APIs.
